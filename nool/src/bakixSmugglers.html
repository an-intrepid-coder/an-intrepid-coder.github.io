Bakix Smugglers

Overview: One of countless smuggling concerns operating out of the city of Jeg. This group of savvy thieves is known to have contacts within the Great Forest of Gih, which gives them their nickname of the "Bakix River Smugglers". In practice, they are small players in Jeg and mostly play a small role in helping people smuggle goods in and out of the city walls, to avoid tariffs between the Docks and the main roads. Most people have never heard of them. Only a character with the Streetwise Skill is likely to know they exist prior to being approached by them, and they are such small players in Jeg that one is likely to be approached by any number of other groups.
