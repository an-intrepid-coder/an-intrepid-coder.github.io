Jeg Town Guard

Overview: A well-funded police force of over 1,000 members. These fellows roam the streets on patrol, in small squads of 3-4, armed with swords and spears; typically clad in mail. There are investigative units and secret police who deal with longer-term and more serious issues, but most people will only ever encounter patrol squads. The Town Guard is largely in the employ of The Merchant's Guild, although their ancient legal mandate is more vague and independent in theory. These folks often serve as the focal point in the ongoing conflict between the Merchant's Guild and innumerable smuggler organizations who flock from around the realm to subvert the infamous tarrifs of Jeg (for both fun and profit). As such, some guards are corrupt or are double- agents.
