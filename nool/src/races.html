Allowed Racial Templates: Only human, for now. Aliens and non-human species may feature later on. Humans in this universe, however, are numerous and infinite in their variety. Aliens, elves, dragons, etc. will probably pop up as the setting progresses. If a player really wants to make a non-human from the start, then the GM will figure something out! When The Crisis begins, affected creatures (the "Zombies") will be using custom templates, and those templates will be listed here along with any playable non-human species which pop up over the course of the campaign. It is possible, for example, that the party may fail the initial campaign and "respawn" somewhere else in the game universe to handle The Crisis from a different angle entirely, using very different characters with different backstories. So, my intent is to start simple and then give the players more options as the setting gets more complex. Elves, cyborgs, aliens -- all potentially possible. Perhaps they will even play "Zombies" at some point? Who knows.
