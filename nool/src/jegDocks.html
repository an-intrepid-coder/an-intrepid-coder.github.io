Jeg Docks

Time Zone: Kingdom of Nool

Overview: The bustling docksides which line the Nem River as it snakes through Jeg are the heart of the city. Most people who live here work at the docks, or cater to people who work at the docks. These docksides span several miles on both sides of the river as it passes through the city, and the city's shape is defined by the massive shipping and warehousing industries which have sprung up around them. While many parts of the docks are very safe, some are not. Bazaars, markets, shops, libraries, inns, smithies, taverns, middle class neighborhoods, industrial zones, Guard Barracks, shrines, temples, jails, slums, graveyards, wealthy manors, Merchant's Guild offices, and ancient family crypts can all be found in the docks. A streetwise adventurer could spend an entire career in this area alone. Someone familiar with the area can generally find their way around from any given point to any other roughly known point within two hours, and often much less. Multiple bridges cross the Nem and connect docksides on either shore.
